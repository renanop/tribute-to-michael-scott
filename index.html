Steve Carell

Former Regional Manager of Scranton Dunder Mifflin.

Here is a time line of great Michael Scott (A.K.A. Steve Carell).

  • 1962 - Born in Concord, Massaschusetts.
  • 1992 - Was hired as a salesman by the Scranton branch of Dunder Mifflin, a medium sized company in the paper business.
  • 1984 - Graduated on Denison University - Ohio (History Bachelor's Degree
  • 1995 - Married Nancy Carell.
  • 2001 - His first child, Elizabeth, is born.
  • 2004 - Second child, John, is born.
  • 2005 - Debuted on the aclamed TV show The Office
  • 2006- Wins the Golden Globe Award for best actor because of his role in The Office.
  • 2009 - Founds the Michael Scott Paper Company INC.
  • 2011 - Leaves the position of Regional Manager at Dunder Mifflin in order to move with his family to Colorado
